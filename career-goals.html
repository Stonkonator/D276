Career Goals

Three-Year Vision

Over the next three years, I have ambitious but achievable career goals that will position me as a leader in the technology industry. My goals reflect my passion for innovation and continuous learning, as well as my desire to create meaningful impact through technology.

Goal 1: Become a Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

My primary career-focused objective is to advance to a Chief Technology Officer role within the next three years. As CTO, I aim to lead technology strategy and innovation for a forward-thinking organization. This position will allow me to leverage my decade of experience in building custom integrations, managing enterprise systems, and developing data-driven solutions to drive organizational success..

I am currently doing the following to achieve this goal:

  • Completing my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Western Governors University
  • Continuously expanding my technical expertise in emerging technologies, including AI, machine learning, and cloud architecture
  • Developing my leadership and strategic planning skills through hands-on management experience
  • Building a strong professional network within the technology leadership community
  • Demonstrating measurable business impact through innovative technology solutions

Goal 2: Launch a Technology Startup in Stock Trading Automation

I plan to create and launch a tech startup focused on stock trading automation, custom technical analysis tools, and market data, leveraging cutting-edge machine learning and artificial intelligence research. This venture will combine my technical expertise in software development, data management, and automation with my passion for financial technology.

The startup will focus on:

  • Developing novel ML/AI model architectures, training approaches, and data management
  • Creating intelligent automation systems for trading strategies
  • Building scalable, secure platforms for retail and institutional traders
  • Providing accessible technology solutions that democratize advanced trading capabilities
  • Securing a positive revenue flow by self-hosting and offering a subscription-based model

This entrepreneurial goal represents my commitment to innovation and my belief in using technology to solve complex problems. By establishing this startup, I will contribute to the advancement of financial technology while creating opportunities for growth and employment in the tech sector.

Commitment to Continuous Growth

In addition to the above, I am also extremely committed to lifelong learning and professional development. I stay current with emerging technologies and research, participate in technical communities on LinkedIn, and continuously seek opportunities to expand my skills.

These goals reflect both my professional ambitions as well as my dedication to making a meaningful impact through technology. I am excited about the journey ahead and the opportunities to contribute to innovation in both corporate and entrepreneurial settings.
